Background

  The idea of this forum is for discussing and guiding the Church to discover the Sound Teaching of the Bible was suggested in a recently concluded conference in Sharjah after a meaningful discussion about the contemporary challenges before the Church at large and Church in Telugu States in particular. It was observed that one of the dire needs in the local churches is Sound Teaching. As there were some individuals who were already discussing about these confusing teachings on the face book, the suggestion was to make it a formal formed.

Purpose

  • “Helping the Church and Society in discovering Sound Biblical Teaching concerning Christian Faith and Conduct based on proper principles of Biblical Interpretation.”

  • Motto:

    • “Unity in Essentials, Liberty in non-essentials, Charity in everything.”

  • To Be:

    • “Watchman studying the trends, Whistle blower exposing error, Warrior guarding the Truth.”

Objectives

  1. Help individuals or groups engage in a serious pursuit of discovering the Biblical Teaching, to learn the Sound Biblical Theology for a Right living.
  2. Identify and address the Contemporary Issues of contention, confusion and corruption that are detrimental to the Biblical Faith and Conduct.
  3. To create a platform for engaging in healthy discussions and learnings to understand the Scripture in a better way applicable to the context, to equip God’s people for works of service, so that the body of Christ may be built up on Right Foundations.
